About Us
We are two teenagers who live in York who have decided to start a car washing company. Our business model is making car washing affordable, easy, accessible and friendly. We book you in and clean your car at your house while you get on with your day. We accept both PayPal and Cash.

We offer a few different services, perfect for every car. Your support by choosing our service really helps us out a lot and keeps this business running.
