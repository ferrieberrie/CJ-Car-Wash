About Us
We are two teenagers who live in York who have decided to start a car washing company. We are so proud about how well our business has grown and the positive feedback we have received from our customers. We have invested in the best equipment and a lot of our time into this business and we are so happy to be able to provide a service that is so appreciated in our local community. Personally, any money I earn will be going towards flying lessons to pursue my dream of becoming a pilot, and I am so grateful for all the support and orders which can help me get there. We look forward to hearing from you and making your car shine!
Important Updates
Prices and booking updates
To improve our services, we now charge a different rate per car, as every car is different and every customer has their own requirements. This new system allows us to provide more accurate and fair pricing for our customers and also help us with managing our timing slots to allocate the right amount of time for each car.
